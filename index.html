Technology is essential to progress.
Art is essential to human experience.

We live in an increasingly digital world; Who we are, who our friends are, what we like, what we don’t, everything is stored and processed online with our personal devices as a gateway. Our lives are determined by our interaction with software and our interaction with others through software.

A computer engineer creates solutions that fit specifications and deliver on expectations, but human interpretation is often overlooked. An artist builds a product that induces emotions and conveys a message. To an artist, understanding interpretation is key to success. Influential software and hardware must be aware of its user and the world it exists in.

Any Solution can fulfill requirements.
Elegant solutions can change the world.
